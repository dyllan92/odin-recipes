Garlic Chilli Chicken

This spicy chicken is great either as a standalone nibble, or as the meatbase of another dish

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons chili oil
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 4 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
  2. Toss chicken pieces lightly in chili oil. Mix garlic powder, chili powder, and salt together well in a bowl. Coat chicken with spice mixture; place coated chicken pieces on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer.
  3. Bake chicken bites in the preheated oven until browned, chicken is no longer pink at the center, and juices run clear, about 8 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the center should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
